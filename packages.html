Signature Experience

Sunrise Balloon Flight

Float above Cappadocia's fairy chimneys and valleys as the sun rises. We arrange your balloon flight with a certified local operator and handle all logistics from the hotel. Prices vary by season and operator — ask our team for current availability.

  • Approx. 1-hour hot air balloon flight
  • Hotel pick-up and drop-off included
  • Flight certificate on landing
  • Champagne or juice toast after landing (operator dependent)
  • Arranged directly through our team — no middlemen

Rooftop Package

Rooftop & Balloons Stay

Two nights in any room of your choice, with daily breakfast on the terrace and guaranteed access to our rooftop during the morning balloon hour. The perfect way to experience Cappadocia's iconic skies without leaving the hotel.

  • 2 nights accommodation (room type of choice)
  • Daily Turkish breakfast buffet included
  • Rooftop terrace access at dawn for balloon viewing
  • Welcome drink on arrival
  • Complimentary local map and activity recommendations

Explorer Package

Valley & ATV Tour

Explore the Rose Valley, Pigeon Valley and Love Valley by ATV — guided routes through Cappadocia's most dramatic landscapes. We organise the tour through a trusted local operator and you depart directly from the hotel area. Suitable for beginners; no experience required.

  • 2-hour guided ATV tour through the valleys
  • Safety briefing and equipment provided
  • Small group sizes (guides on ATVs with you)
  • Hotel drop-off after the tour
  • Can be combined with balloon flight on request

Culture & Flavour

Taste of Cappadocia

Discover the culture and crafts of Cappadocia beyond the landscape. Visit a traditional pottery workshop in Avanos — one of Turkey's most famous pottery villages — and explore the underground cities and rock-cut churches of the region with a local guide.

  • Guided Göreme Open Air Museum visit
  • Avanos pottery workshop (hands-on session)
  • Underground city tour (Derinkuyu or Kaymaklı)
  • Transportation arranged from hotel
  • Daily breakfast included
