Our Rooms

Rooms & Suites

Classic Cave

Cave Room

Carved directly into volcanic tufa rock, these rooms offer the most authentic cave experience in Cappadocia. The rounded, textured walls and low vaulted ceiling create a naturally insulated, cocoon-like atmosphere — cool in summer, warm in winter. Furnished with a comfortable double bed, warm wall sconces and essential amenities.

Double Bed Volcanic Tufa Walls Flat-screen TV En-suite Shower Electric Kettle Mini Fridge Free Wi-Fi Central Heating Hair Dryer Toiletries Breakfast Included
From €75 / night
Stone Room

Stone Room

Built with hand-cut Cappadocian limestone, these rooms have a warm, golden character. Exposed stone block walls rise to a wooden carved beamed ceiling with recessed spotlights. Each room features a wooden dressing table with mirror, electric kettle, flat-screen TV, and an en-suite stone-tiled bathroom with walk-in shower.

Double Bed Limestone Stone Walls Wooden Beamed Ceiling Flat-screen TV En-suite Shower Electric Kettle Mini Fridge Free Wi-Fi Central Heating Hair Dryer Toiletries Breakfast Included
From €90 / night
Superior

Superior Stone Room

A larger stone room offering extra space and a dedicated in-room dining table with two chairs — ideal for guests staying longer or those who appreciate room to unwind. The same golden limestone walls and wooden beamed ceiling as the standard stone room, with a slightly elevated sleeping area in select rooms. Equipped with flat-screen TV, wooden dressing table, electric kettle and mini fridge.

Double Bed Stone Walls & Beamed Ceiling In-room Dining Table Flat-screen TV En-suite Shower Electric Kettle Mini Fridge Free Wi-Fi Central Heating Hair Dryer Breakfast Included
From €110 / night
Traditional Suite

Traditional Suite

Our most spacious and ornate room, the Traditional Suite celebrates Cappadocian artistry. A richly carved wooden ceiling, kilim rug underfoot and a dedicated sitting area with sofa create a generous living environment. Traditional textile drapes, warm ambient lighting and stone walls complete the atmosphere. The en-suite bathroom features a walk-in shower and full toiletries.
